# 🏛️ Civic Decoder Kenya

Kenya legislative and civic intelligence — MP voting records, CDF spending,
bill tracking for the 13th Parliament (2022–2027).

[![CI](https://github.com/gabrielmahia/civic-decoder/actions/workflows/ci.yml/badge.svg)](https://github.com/gabrielmahia/civic-decoder/actions)

**Live:** [civic-decoder.streamlit.app](https://civic-decoder.streamlit.app)

## What it does

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 👤 MP Tracker | Attendance %, bills sponsored, questions asked, CDF absorption per MP |
| 📜 Bill Tracker | Bill status, votes, plain-language summaries |
| 💰 CDF Watchdog | Constituency fund utilisation ranked — which MPs are spending and who isn't |

## Data sources

All data is public domain from official government sources:
- [parliament.go.ke](https://parliament.go.ke) — MP records, Hansard, bills
- [NG-CDF Annual Reports](https://ngcdf.go.ke/annual-reports/) — fund utilisation
- [Kenya Gazette](https://kenyagazette.go.ke) — enacted legislation

Seed dataset: 15 MPs, 10 bills, 14 CDF records. Community contributions expand coverage.
